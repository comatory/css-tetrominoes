CSS tetrominos

The design is taken from original Gameboy release of Tetris in 1989. This spritesheet was used as an inspiration.

Shapes are ("I"|"J"|"L"|"O"|"S"|"T"|"Z") and are displayed with initial spawn rotation at 0 degrees - defined by SRS system.
